# Size Guide

This guide covers fit and sizing recommendations for every brand we carry. When in doubt, **size up** — it's easier to exchange for a smaller size than a larger one.

---

## Clothing

### Skims

Skims sizing is designed to stretch and adapt to your body. General guidance:

- **Fits Everybody line** (bodysuits, underwear): True to size. The fabric stretches 3x, so fit your usual size. If between sizes, go down.
- **Soft Lounge line** (dresses, sets): Size down. The stretch fabric can look baggy if you go TTS.
- **Cozy line** (sweats, knits): True to size or size up for an oversized fit.
- **Cotton line**: True to size, minimal stretch.

### Arc'teryx

Arc'teryx fits **slim by design** — intentionally close to the body to reduce bulk under a pack or harness.

- **If you wear a medium in most brands**: size up to Large in Arc'teryx
- **If you have broad shoulders or a muscular build**: size up two sizes
- **Women's styles**: run true to women's sizing but slim through the torso

Arc'teryx Beta AR Jacket specific notes:
- Designed to layer over a midlayer (fleece or down). If you plan to layer underneath, size up one.
- Helmet-compatible hood — the hood fits over most climbing helmets at size M+.

### Levi's

- **501 Jeans**: True to size in waist. The 501 has a relaxed seat and thigh — if you prefer a slimmer fit, try the 511.
- Inseam lengths: 28", 30", 32", 34". Most people 5'4"–5'8" wear a 30" or 32".

---

### Uniqlo

Uniqlo uses **Japanese sizing**, which tends to run **one size smaller** than US sizing.

- **Ultra Light Down Jacket**: Size up one from your usual US size. A US Medium typically needs a Uniqlo Large.

## Footwear

### Nike (Air Max 270)
- True to size for most people. If you have wide feet, go up half a size.
- Available in full and half sizes.

### Adidas (Ultraboost 24)
- True to size. The Primeknit upper stretches slightly — do not size up.
- Available in full and half sizes.

### Allbirds (Tree Runner)
- Allbirds runs **true to size** but narrow. If you have wide feet, size up half a size.
- Available in whole sizes only — half sizes should round down (Allbirds recommends this).

### Timberland (6-Inch Boot)
- Timberlands run **half a size large**. Size down half a size from your usual.
- Available in D (standard) and W (wide) widths.

### New Balance (990v6)
- True to size. Available in an unusually wide range of widths: N (narrow), D (standard), 2E (wide), 4E (extra wide).
- If you have wide feet, New Balance 990v6 in 2E is one of the best options available.

## General Sizing Tips

- **When between sizes**: For structured items (denim, hard-shell jackets, boots), size down. For stretch items (Skims, Alo), trust the size — the stretch does the work.
- **Activewear**: Should feel snug but not restrictive. If you can't do a full squat comfortably, size up.
- **Outerwear**: Always consider what you'll layer underneath. A fleece or thick sweater adds 1–2 sizes of bulk.
- **Returns**: We offer free size exchanges on clothing and footwear within 30 days.
