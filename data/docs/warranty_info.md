# Warranty Information

## Overview

Most products include a manufacturer's warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Warranties do **not** cover accidental damage, misuse, or normal wear and tear.

For defective items within 30 days of delivery, contact us first — we'll handle it directly. After 30 days, contact the manufacturer using the information below.

---

## Electronics

### Apple (MacBook Pro, iPad Pro, AirPods Pro)

- **Standard warranty**: 1 year limited warranty against manufacturing defects
- **AppleCare+**: Extends to 2 years + covers 2 incidents of accidental damage per year ($99–$299 depending on product)
- **Battery coverage**: Apple replaces batteries that drop below 80% capacity within warranty period
- **How to claim**: apple.com/support or visit an Apple Store. Serial number required.
- **Tip**: Register your Apple product at appleid.apple.com immediately after purchase.

### Sony (WH-1000XM6 Headphones)

- **Standard warranty**: 1 year limited warranty
- **What's covered**: Manufacturing defects, driver failure, charging port issues
- **What's not covered**: Physical damage, water damage beyond IPX4 rating, ear cushion wear
- **How to claim**: pro.sony.com/bbsc/ssr/support-home — select your product and "Repair & Warranty"

### Samsung (QLED TV)

- **Standard warranty**: 1 year parts and labor
- **Panel warranty**: Samsung covers panel defects for 1 year. Dead pixels covered if more than 5 are present.
- **How to claim**: samsung.com/us/support or call 1-800-SAMSUNG

### Dell (XPS 13)

- **Standard warranty**: 1 year basic onsite service
- **Premium Support**: Upgradeable to 3–5 years. Includes 24/7 phone support and next-business-day onsite repair.
- **How to claim**: dell.com/support — enter your Service Tag (found on bottom of laptop)

## Home Appliances

### Dyson (V15 Detect)

- **Standard warranty**: 2 years parts and labor
- **Battery**: Covered for 2 years. Dyson will replace batteries that fail under normal use within warranty.
- **Filters**: HEPA filters are consumables — not covered under warranty. Replace every 12 months.
- **How to claim**: dyson.com/support or call 1-866-MY-DYSON. Register your machine at dyson.com for faster service.

### Instant Pot (Duo 7-in-1)

- **Standard warranty**: 1 year limited warranty (lid, inner pot, and electrical components)
- **Sealing ring**: Considered a consumable — not covered. Replace every 12–18 months or when it retains odors.
- **How to claim**: instantpot.com/support — email support with proof of purchase and photos

## What Warranties Don't Cover

Across all brands, the following are universally **excluded** from warranty coverage:

- Accidental damage (drops, spills, impact)
- Water damage beyond stated IP/water resistance ratings
- Normal wear and tear (scratched surfaces, faded colors, compressed insulation)
- Consumable parts (batteries after warranty period, vacuum filters, sealing rings)
- Damage from improper use, storage, or cleaning
- Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect function
