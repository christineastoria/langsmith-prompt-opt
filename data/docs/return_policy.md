# Return & Exchange Policy

## Overview

We want you to love everything you buy. If something isn't right, we make returns easy.

**Standard return window: 30 days from delivery date.**

Items must be:
- In original, unworn/unused condition
- With all original tags attached
- In original packaging where applicable

---

## Return Windows by Category

| Category | Return Window | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| Electronics | 30 days | Must be unopened OR opened but unused. All accessories, cables, and documentation must be included. |
| Clothing & Activewear | 30 days | Unworn, unwashed, tags attached. No deodorant marks, perfume, or visible wear. |
| Footwear | 30 days | Unworn, in original box. Shoes tried on indoors only (no outdoor wear). |
| Beauty & Skincare | 14 days | Unopened and sealed only. We cannot accept returns on opened beauty products for hygiene reasons. |
| Candles | 14 days | Unlit and in original packaging only. |
| Fragrances | 14 days | Unopened and sealed. |
| Home & Appliances | 30 days | In original packaging with all accessories. |
| Drinkware | 30 days | Unused, in original packaging. |

---

## Non-Returnable Items

The following **cannot be returned**:

- Opened beauty products (skincare, makeup, fragrance)
- Lit or burned candles
- Final sale items (marked at checkout)
- Gift cards
- Bodysuits and swimwear (for hygiene reasons) — **this includes Skims bodysuits**
- Items returned after the return window

---

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Go to your Order History and select the item
2. Select "Start a Return" and choose a reason
3. Print the prepaid return label (free returns on orders over $50)
4. Drop off at any UPS or USPS location within 7 days of initiating

Refunds are processed within **5-7 business days** of receiving the return. Original shipping is non-refundable.

---

## Exchanges

We offer **direct exchanges** on clothing and footwear for size or color within 30 days. Initiate the same way as a return and select "Exchange" instead.

Beauty, candles, and electronics are **exchange-only if defective**, not for preference.

---

## Defective or Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within **48 hours of delivery** with photos. We'll send a replacement immediately at no cost. No need to return the defective item for orders under $100.

---

## Late Returns

Returns initiated after 30 days (14 days for beauty/candles) are reviewed case-by-case. We may offer store credit instead of a refund. Returns after 60 days are not accepted under any circumstances.

---

## Brand-Specific Notes

**Apple products:** Apple has its own 14-day return window from purchase date for opened products. Unopened Apple products follow our standard 30-day window.

**Arc'teryx:** All Arc'teryx gear comes with a manufacturer's lifetime warranty against defects. Warranty claims go through Arc'teryx directly at warranty.arcteryx.com.

**Dyson:** Dyson products include a 2-year manufacturer's warranty. Defective Dyson units can be returned within 30 days; after that, contact Dyson support directly.

**Diptyque:** Burned or lit candles cannot be returned. Unlit candles in original packaging accepted within 14 days.

**Skims:** Bodysuits cannot be returned for hygiene reasons. All other Skims items follow the standard 30-day policy.

**Alo Yoga:** Standard 30-day return. Items must be unworn with tags. Alo does not accept returns on worn activewear regardless of wash.
